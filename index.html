What ZCUS Alberta Does

ZCUS Alberta helps people find community, preserve cultural ties, and participate fully in Alberta civic, family, and economic life.

Newcomer Support

Welcoming guidance, community referrals, settlement navigation, and friendly connections for people building a life in Alberta.

Culture and Belonging

Events, storytelling, food, music, language, and shared traditions that keep Zimbabwean identity visible and celebrated.

Youth and AIFY

Mentorship, leadership pathways, funding support, and youth-focused spaces through AIFY and related community efforts.

Members and Partners

Volunteer opportunities, partner collaboration, and a growing network of people who care about strong communities.

The spirit of Zimbabwe meets the heart of Alberta

ZCUS Alberta exists so community members can stay connected to heritage while building a strong future here. The society is rooted in unity, mutual support, cultural pride, and practical action.

Who this is for

A place for every generation

Programs and gatherings are shaped for newcomers, seniors, youth, families, business owners, volunteers, members, partners, and funders.

  • New residents looking for a welcoming point of connection.
  • Parents and youth building confidence across cultures.
  • Seniors seeking friendship, care, and community presence.
  • Business owners and professionals who want to contribute.
  • Funders and partners investing in inclusive community capacity.

Partners and Funders

ZCUS Alberta is ready to work with organizations that support settlement, youth leadership, senior connection, cultural programming, and inclusive community capacity.

Community partners

Collaborate on referrals, venue access, outreach, event support, and services that help people find the right help faster.

Funders and sponsors

Support practical programming with clear community benefit, including youth mentorship, newcomer support, and cultural events.

Accountable delivery

Work with a society focused on visible community outcomes, volunteer contribution, and respectful stewardship.
