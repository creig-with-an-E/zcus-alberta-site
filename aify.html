Youth program

AIFY helps young people move forward

AIFY is presented as a youth-focused pathway for mentorship, leadership, funding awareness, and confidence building in community.

What AIFY Can Support

The program can help youth build networks, understand opportunities, and connect with mentors who care about their goals.

Mentorship

Connect youth with adults and peers who can share experience, encouragement, and practical guidance.

Funding awareness

Help youth and families understand available supports, eligibility, and next steps when opportunities are open.

Leadership

Create pathways for young people to serve, speak, organize, and grow within the community.

For youth, parents, mentors, and partners

AIFY works best when families, volunteers, and funders contribute together.

  • Youth can ask about current openings, mentorship, and leadership activities.
  • Parents and guardians can contact the society for program details and eligibility.
  • Mentors can volunteer time, professional experience, and encouragement.
  • Partners and funders can support youth participation and program capacity.
