About the society

Unity, support, and cultural connection

ZCUS Alberta is a nonprofit community organization serving Zimbabweans and friends of Zimbabwe across Alberta.

The spirit of Zimbabwe meets the heart of Alberta

That story guides ZCUS Alberta. The society creates welcoming spaces where people can celebrate heritage, find practical support, and build relationships that make Alberta feel like home.

Mission

Bring people together

ZCUS Alberta promotes unity, mutual support, cultural pride, and community participation among Zimbabweans in Alberta.

Approach

Practical and relational

The work is rooted in real needs: settlement support, cultural events, youth mentorship, senior connection, and volunteer service.

Community Values

These values shape the programs, partnerships, and everyday welcome of the society.

Belonging

People should feel seen, welcomed, and connected across language, age, and background.

Service

Members support one another through time, care, practical help, and trusted referrals.

Culture

Zimbabwean heritage is celebrated as a living source of identity, resilience, and joy.

Help shape the next chapter

Membership and volunteer service keep the society responsive, visible, and strong.
