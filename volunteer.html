Volunteer

Your time can make the welcome stronger

Volunteers help ZCUS Alberta run events, support newcomers, mentor youth, check in on seniors, and keep community programs moving.

Ways to Help

There is room for practical, relational, creative, and professional contributions.

Events

Set up, welcome guests, coordinate food, support registration, and help gatherings run smoothly.

Newcomer support

Share local knowledge, make introductions, and help people find trusted resources.

Youth mentorship

Support AIFY, career conversations, leadership activities, and confidence building.

Operations

Help with communications, planning, grant support, partner outreach, and member coordination.

Good volunteer matches

Tell the society what you enjoy, what skills you bring, and how often you can help.

  • One-time event support for busy seasons.
  • Ongoing program support for youth, newcomers, or seniors.
  • Professional skills such as finance, communications, technology, design, or grant writing.
  • Community ambassador support for outreach and partner relationships.
